Find Us

The counter opens early and the coffee is always on.

Where We Are

Wildflour Bakery & the Bakehouse Loft
12 Orchard Lane
Milltown, NY 10101

Look for the wheat-sheaf sign at the foot of Orchard Lane; the barn sits behind the bakery, past the orchard rows. Parking is in the gravel lot beside the meadow — during sugaring season the lot can get muddy, and overflow parking sits behind the Milltown feed store, a three-minute walk up the lane.

How to Reach Us

For bake-schedule questions, supper club seats, or anything bread-shaped, call the counter:

(555) 010-0100

For weddings, retreats, and gatherings in the Bakehouse Loft, write to us — include your hoped-for season and a rough guest count, and Theo will reply with open dates and a walkthrough time:

hello@wildflourbakery.example

We answer every note ourselves, usually between the morning bake and the supper service, so allow a day or two. If you are writing about a wedding, the walkthrough is the real beginning — come stand in the barn and picture it.

Visiting the Loft

The Bakehouse Loft is open by appointment and on supper nights. If the lights are on and the big door is rolled back, you are welcome to poke your head in — unless there is a wedding underway, in which case the bread counter will gladly keep you company instead.

Start With a Loaf

Every gathering here started with somebody buying bread. Come by the counter and say hello.
